About Me

My name is Esmeralda, though I warmly invite everyone to call me Esme. I hail from the vibrant and industrious port city of Tema, Ghana, a place I am proud to call home and where I have lived my entire life.

Professionally, I have found my calling as a dedicated teacher at Mattmeral Academy. My passion lies in guiding young pupils to discover and appreciate the critical role science plays in understanding and preserving our delicate ecosystem.

When I'm not in the classroom, I enjoy unwinding by watching movies and cherishing quality time playing games with my two wonderful siblings.

Tema, Ghana

Ghana, a vibrant nation on the West African coast, is renowned for its rich history, cultural warmth, and significant natural wealth.

It gained fame as the former Gold Coast and made history as the first sub-Saharan African country to achieve independence from colonial rule in 1957.

Today, it is a stable democracy celebrated for its friendly people, diverse ethnic groups, and bustling cities like Accra and Kumasi. From its palm-fringed beaches and historic slave castles to its thriving cocoa industry and recent oil discoveries, Ghana stands as a beacon of progress and potential on the continent.
